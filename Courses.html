Courses Offered

Basaveshwar Engineering College, Bagalkot offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and skill-based programs designed to meet the evolving demands of industry, research, and society. The academic programs are structured to provide a strong theoretical foundation, hands-on practical exposure, interdisciplinary learning, and professional competence.

The institution follows an outcome-based education framework that focuses not only on academic excellence but also on critical thinking, ethical responsibility, innovation, and lifelong learning. Each program is periodically reviewed and updated to align with emerging technologies, industry trends, and national education standards.

Undergraduate Programs

The undergraduate programs aim to build strong engineering fundamentals, analytical thinking, and practical problem-solving abilities. These programs prepare students for professional careers, higher studies, research, and entrepreneurship.

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) / B.Tech

The Bachelor of Engineering / Bachelor of Technology programs are four-year full-time professional degrees that focus on developing technical competence, engineering ethics, teamwork, and leadership skills. Students undergo rigorous coursework combined with laboratory work, mini-projects, industry internships, and final-year capstone projects.

  • Computer Science and Engineering

    This program focuses on the design, development, and application of computer systems and software. Core areas include programming, data structures, algorithms, operating systems, databases, computer networks, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. Graduates are prepared for careers in software development, data science, cloud computing, and research.

  • Electronics and Communication Engineering

    The Electronics and Communication Engineering program emphasizes electronic circuits, communication systems, signal processing, embedded systems, VLSI design, and wireless technologies. Students gain practical exposure through advanced laboratories and industry-oriented projects, enabling careers in telecom, semiconductor, and embedded industries.

  • Mechanical Engineering

    Mechanical Engineering provides knowledge in design, manufacturing, thermal sciences, materials, and automation. The program trains students to design mechanical systems, analyze thermal processes, and manage manufacturing operations. Graduates find opportunities in core industries, automotive sectors, energy systems, and research organizations.

  • Civil Engineering

    Civil Engineering focuses on infrastructure development, including structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation, environmental engineering, and construction management. The program emphasizes sustainable development, safety standards, and practical site experience, preparing graduates for public and private sector roles.

  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering

    This program deals with electrical systems, power generation, transmission, control systems, and renewable energy technologies. Students gain expertise in power electronics, electrical machines, and smart grid technologies, enabling careers in power utilities, automation industries, and research institutions.

  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

    The Artificial Intelligence and Data Science program is designed to meet the growing demand for intelligent systems and data-driven decision-making. It covers machine learning, deep learning, data analytics, big data technologies, and real-world AI applications. Graduates are equipped for roles in analytics, AI development, and research-driven industries.

Postgraduate Programs

Postgraduate programs are aimed at advanced specialization, research orientation, and leadership development. These programs enable students to deepen their technical expertise and contribute to innovation and academic research.

Master of Engineering (M.E) / M.Tech

  • Structural Engineering

    This specialization focuses on advanced structural analysis, design of complex structures, earthquake engineering, and sustainable construction practices. Graduates contribute to infrastructure planning, consultancy, and research.

  • VLSI Design

    The VLSI Design program provides in-depth knowledge of integrated circuit design, semiconductor devices, and electronic fabrication technologies. Students gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools, preparing them for careers in chip design and semiconductor industries.

  • Machine Design

    This program emphasizes advanced mechanical design concepts, material selection, finite element analysis, and product development. Graduates are prepared for design engineering roles and research careers.

  • Computer Science and Engineering

    The postgraduate CSE program focuses on advanced computing concepts, including distributed systems, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and advanced algorithms, preparing students for high-end technical and research roles.

In addition to technical programs, the college offers interdisciplinary management and application-oriented degrees that complement engineering education.

  • MBA (Master of Business Administration)

    The MBA program develops managerial, leadership, and entrepreneurial skills. It covers finance, marketing, human resources, operations, and strategic management, preparing graduates for leadership roles in corporate and public sectors.

  • MCA (Master of Computer Applications)

    The MCA program focuses on advanced software development, application design, and IT management. It bridges computing concepts with practical application development for industry needs.

Diploma Programs

Diploma programs are designed to provide technical skills and practical knowledge at an early stage. These programs emphasize hands-on training, workshop practice, and industry-relevant competencies.

  • Diploma in Civil Engineering

    Focuses on basic construction techniques, surveying, materials testing, and site supervision.

  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

    Emphasizes workshop practice, manufacturing processes, and basic mechanical design principles.

  • Diploma in Electrical Engineering

    Covers electrical installation, maintenance, and power system fundamentals.

Skill Development & Industry Programs

To bridge the gap between academia and industry, the college offers structured skill development and industry-oriented programs that enhance employability and innovation.

  • Internship & Industry Training

    Students undergo internships and industrial training to gain real-world exposure, understand professional work environments, and apply theoretical knowledge to practical challenges.

  • Research & Innovation Projects

    Research projects encourage students to explore new ideas, develop prototypes, and contribute to technological advancement under faculty mentorship.

  • Certification Courses in Emerging Technologies

    Certification programs in AI, data science, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and automation help students stay current with industry demands.

  • Entrepreneurship Development Programs

    Entrepreneurship programs foster startup culture, innovation, and business thinking, encouraging students to become job creators rather than job seekers.
